2 . TICKETS
> 2-1 What does the ticket include?

With the ticket, you can participate in all the activities in TRANS, including Keynote Speeches, Panel Talks and Booth Alley.

> 2-2 How much is the ticket?

More detail ticket price

> 2-3 Where can I purchase tickets?

Enter the Accupass website. And please sign up/in to be the member of Accupass, and you can register for TRANS.

> 2-4 How to pay for the ticket?

(1) Credit Card ─ VISA/Master/JCB/Union Pay
(2) LINE Pay
(3) ATM Transfer
(4) Family Mart Payment-FamiPort Code

> 2-5 Do we have the deadline of the ticket payment?

You have two days paying for the tickets. After two days, you need to order the ticket again.

> 2-6 Can I ask for receipt or tax ID when purchasing the ticket?

Yes, please fill in the requests when you register for TRANS

> 2-7 How to get the ticket after purchasing?

(1) Sign in Accupass, search ”My Tickets”, and you can see the ticket.
(2) You will get a SMS, click the website included and you can get the ticket.
(3) Get the ticket via Email.

> 2-8 How can I confirm that I register successfully?

After you successfully register for TRANS, you will receive the e-ticket via Email and SMS.

> 2-9 How to register for TRANS?

You can buy up to ten tickets in one purchase.

> 2-10 Is the ticket transferable?

Yes, please send us an Email (trans.ticket@ylhealth.org), with the info. of transferred person (Name, Email, Mobile phone, Company/Organization, Title, Nationality, The organization's primary industry)

> 2-11 Can I get refund the ticket?

No, please transfer the ticket to your friend interested in TRANS.

> 2-12 Does the ticket include lunch or dinner?

No, but we offer food map of the neighborhood close to the venue for you to go on a cuisine journey!

> 2-13 If I am not local, how can I pay for the ticket?

You can pay for the ticket with credit cards. Please be reminded that different banks may ask for different charges.
